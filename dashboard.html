Meet the Trainer

With generations of equestrian heritage, our head trainer brings a wealth of knowledge, patience, and passion to Alvarez - Roman Heritage Equestrian. We believe in building a foundation of absolute trust and mutual respect between horse and rider.

Whether you are stepping into the stirrups for the first time or competing at an elite level, our personalized training programs are meticulously designed to unlock your full potential while honoring the timeless, classical traditions of horsemanship.

Interested in bringing your horse to be trained?

We offer specialized training to meet your horse's needs across a variety of equestrian disciplines. Our training programs welcome both beginner and more advanced horses aiming for top levels of achievement.

From young to mature horses, inexperienced to experienced horses, and small to bigger horses, Demetrio Alvarez will create the perfect program for your horse to ensure they learn and progress.
