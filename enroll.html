Enrolment Information

Our program is specialized for all horses to develop natural athletic ability. We specialize in perfecting foundations that prepare horses for any discipline, ensuring they are balanced, responsive, and confident. Every horse is different and requires their own specialized program to ensure they perform at their highest.

What We Teach

  • Basic and Advanced Ground Work
  • Basic and Advanced Foundation
  • Many Disciplines

Common Questions (FAQ)

How long will my horse be in training?

While every horse is different, it might take 3 - 6 months to develop a solid foundation and gain trust and respect.

Can I visit my horse during training?

Absolutely. We encourage owners to visit at a scheduled time to see progress and learn what is being taught.

What age is required to start training?

Horses of any age are welcome to start training.
